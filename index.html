Know your Akan name

This is a special application that helps the user know the day they where born and their Akan names

What is this Akan names? Akan namesare derived from Ghanian culture.

Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.

With that fill in the form below if you are curious to know about your can name.

Enter your birthday

Enter your birthday in the calendar format (mm/dd/yy):
  • dd represents a day
  • mm represents month
  • y represents year

Choose your gender

Choose your gender on choose your gender field because akan name will be given according to both birthday and gender.

Submit button

Finally, click on submit button identified as AKAN NAME MYSTERY BUTTON to get the day you born on and your akan name.
